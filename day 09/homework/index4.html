What is Halloween


Halloween is celebrated on October 31st and has roots in ancient Celtic traditions,
particularly the festival of Samhain. During Samhain, people believed the boundary between
the living and the dead blurred, and they lit
bonfires and wore costumes to ward off spirits. Over time,
Halloween evolved into a day of fun activities like trick-or-treating, carving pumpkins into jack-o'-lanterns,
and dressing up in spooky or creative costumes.

  8. Visiting Haunted Attractions: Many visit haunted
    houses or participate in ghost tours for a thrill.
