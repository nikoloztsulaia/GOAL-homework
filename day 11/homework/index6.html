history of apple

Apple was started in 1976 by Steve Jobs, Steve Wozniak,
and Ronald Wayne. They worked in a garage to create their first computer, the Apple I. Later,
they released the Apple II, which made Apple very popular. In 1984, Apple launched the Macintosh,
the first computer with a mouse and screen that looked like modern ones. However, the company faced problems, and in 1997, Jobs came back to lead it.
He helped create the colorful iMac and other cool products. Apple really became
famous with the iPod (music player) in 2001 and the iPhone (smartphone) in 2007.
These gadgets changed how people listen to music, use phones, and even live their lives. Now, Apple is one of the biggest
companies in the world, known for its stylish products like
the iPad, Apple Watch, and MacBooks.
